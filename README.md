This dynamic dashboard provides a comprehensive, visually stunning, and immediate overview of key performance indicators (KPIs) for the Amit Ecommerce platform, allowing users to track and analyze sales performance across multiple dimensions. Built using Power BI, the dashboard utilizes a sleek, dark-themed design to enhance readability and focus.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard instantly highlights critical business health metrics:

Sum of Amount: 438K

Sum of Quantity: 5615

Sum of Profit: 37K

Sum of AOV (Average Order Value): 121K

Core Insights and Visualizations
The dashboard is structured around several insightful visualizations:

Profit by Month: A bar chart displays monthly profit trends, clearly showing periods of high performance (e.g., February, March, December) and months where the business incurred losses (e.g., June, November).

Sum of Quantity by Payment Mode: A donut chart breaks down the quantity of sales by the payment methods used, revealing the most and least preferred modes.

Profit by Sub-Category (Geographic): A horizontal bar chart compares profitability across different regions, with Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh being the top-performing states.

Sum of Amount by Customer Name: A bar chart identifies the top-spending customers, with Harivansh and Madhav being the leaders in total purchase amount.
